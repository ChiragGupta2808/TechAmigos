About Us

Introduction to the club

With a motive to provide a common platform for Interdisciplinary technical activities of the college, Tech Amigos Club was launched. The club consists of self-motivated students who gather together to come up with some or the other new and innovative ideas and work on them. It aims to technically strengthen the students by integrating their skills in the various fields of Engineering & Technology, so as to cope up with the highly competitive environment. The objective is to develop synergies among student creating platform for the student and by the student in terms of research, technical events, workshop etc. Also it will help in identifying potential innovators for betterment of the society. This club is basically formed to enhance skill level of student other than regular curriculum and give benefits to student who wants to do more activity with their talent in the field of computer technology & networking. Club also focuses on personality development programs through live activities from industrial environment. Tech Amigos club provides platform to the students to showcase their talent in the field of Technology and opportunities to gain knowledge and expertise in the field. Club also conducts tech events in the college on regular basis which will include various competitions, webinars, boot-camps and project related to new technologies. Being a part of a Tech Amigos club will offer mentoring programs that can help with knowledge, career advice, and networking opportunities. Working one-on-one with a mentor is especially beneficial for students who struggle with the technical side of engineering. Joining this club will also give you the opportunity to meet a diverse population of people that allow you to expand your thinking and interest.
